Delete Your RunwayRide Account

RunwayRide allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data at any time. If you wish to permanently delete your account, you can do so by contacting our customer support through the mobile application.

How to Request Account Deletion

Follow these steps inside the RunwayRide mobile application:

  • Open the RunwayRide mobile application
  • Go to your Profile section
  • Select Customer Support / Help & Support
  • Raise a request for Account Deletion
  • Our support team will review your request and process the deletion

Request Deletion via Call Support

Users can also request account deletion by contacting the support team through the Call Support option available inside the RunwayRide mobile application.

Our support team will verify your account details and initiate the account deletion process.

What Happens After Requesting Deletion

  • Your account will be permanently deleted after verification.
  • Your personal information associated with the account will be removed.
  • You will no longer be able to access RunwayRide services with that account.

Need Help?

If you need assistance, please contact RunwayRide support using the Help & Support section available inside the mobile application.
